The Workout Log

Log your sets, weight, and reps for your exercises in order to push yourself each time you visit the gym. Store your body weight and progress photos in one place to keep yourself motivated. Switch easily between different workout routines and days depending on your needs or fitness goals. Watch your lifts increase with custom graphs showing weight for each rep and total weight for the set.

App Features

The Workout Log is a simple customizable log for your workouts.

Your Routines

Use one of the provided routines or quickly create your own custom routine. Routines are made up of days, exercises, and sets. The possibilities are endless.

Record Your Stats

Push yourself further in your workouts by quickly viewing your past weight, repetitions, and notes so you can steadily increase your strenght.

Set Your Timer

Rest efficiently using the built in timer to alert you when your rest period is over. Set the timer to count up when doing a timed exercise or when keeping track of cardio.

Go Even Futher
With a History of
Your Workouts.

By purchasing the history feature you can quickly view a full history of your routine, view your workout data plotted out to clearly demonstrate your progress over time, and ensure sustained development to the app over time.

Privacy Policy

The Workout Log doesn't collect any exercise data or images. All data input in the app is stored locally on the device. The Workout Log uses Crashlytics(i.e. Google) in order to track activity in the app and be notified about how and why crashes occur.
